Meet Juliana Maduforo

Neuropsychotherapist & Registered Nurse

I am a Neuropsychotherapist and Registered Nurse specializing in PTSD, clinical trauma, OCD, ADHD, anxiety, and depression. With additional training in marriage, couple, and family therapy, as well as postgraduate certifications in trauma recovery, I bring a holistic and integrative approach to healing.

My work combines biology, psychology, and spirituality to help individuals and groups find meaning, build healthier relationships, and reclaim hope after pain.
