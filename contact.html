Common Questions

How quickly will I hear back from you?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours (Monday-Friday). If you don't hear from us within this timeframe, please check your spam folder or try contacting us again.

Can I schedule an appointment through this contact form?

While you can express interest in scheduling through this form, we recommend using our dedicated Book Appointment page for the fastest scheduling experience. You can also call us directly to schedule.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer a complimentary 15-30 minute phone consultation to discuss your needs, answer questions, and determine if our services are a good fit. You can schedule this through our appointments page.

How do I book Julie for a speaking engagement?

Use this contact form and select "Speaking Engagement" as your inquiry type. Include details about your event (date, location, audience, topic of interest) and we'll follow up to discuss availability and logistics.

Is my information kept confidential?

Yes. All contact information and inquiries are kept confidential. We will never share your information with third parties. For established therapy clients, all communications are protected under HIPAA privacy laws.

What if I need to reach you urgently?

For established clients with urgent (non-emergency) needs, please call our office number and leave a detailed message. We'll respond as quickly as possible during business hours. For true emergencies, please call 911 or use the emergency resources listed above.
