Our Services

Comprehensive healing services for individuals, groups, and organizations

Holistic Care for Your Whole Person

At Sacred Space Therapeutic Ministry, we offer evidence-based, compassionate services that integrate biological, psychological, and spiritual dimensions of healing.

Whether you're seeking individual therapy, group workshops, speaking engagements, or organizational training, our approach honors your unique journey while drawing on the latest research in neuropsychotherapy and trauma recovery.

Individual Therapy

Personalized, evidence-based therapeutic care specializing in trauma, PTSD, OCD, ADHD, anxiety, and depression. Using neuropsychotherapy and trauma-informed approaches, we work together to understand your unique experience and create pathways to healing.

Specialized Treatment Areas

PTSD & Clinical Trauma

Evidence-based treatment for post-traumatic stress disorder and complex trauma. Using EMDR therapy, somatic approaches, and neuropsychotherapy to process traumatic memories and restore a sense of safety.

OCD (Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder)

Specialized treatment for OCD using exposure and response prevention (ERP), cognitive behavioral therapy, and neuropsychological approaches to reduce compulsions and intrusive thoughts.

ADHD Assessment & Treatment

Comprehensive assessment and treatment planning for ADHD in adults and adolescents. Focus on executive function skills, emotional regulation, and practical strategies for daily life.

Anxiety Disorders

Treatment for generalized anxiety, panic disorder, social anxiety, and phobias. Learn to understand your nervous system's responses and develop tools for regulation and resilience.

Depression & Mood Disorders

Compassionate care for depression, addressing biological, psychological, and spiritual dimensions. Develop understanding, hope, and practical strategies for healing.

EMDR Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) is a powerful, evidence-based approach for processing traumatic memories and reducing emotional distress.

Couple & Family Therapy

With additional training in marriage, couple, and family therapy, I offer relationship counseling that addresses patterns, communication, and healing within the context of close relationships. Work together to build healthier connections and navigate challenges with compassion.

Telehealth Sessions

Secure, HIPAA-compliant video sessions available for your convenience. Access quality care from the comfort of your own space.
