About Juliana Maduforo

Neuropsychotherapist, Registered Nurse & Holistic Healer

A Journey of Healing & Purpose

I am a Neuropsychotherapist and Registered Nurse specializing in PTSD, clinical trauma, OCD, ADHD, anxiety, and depression. With additional training in marriage, couple, and family therapy, as well as postgraduate certifications in trauma recovery, I bring a holistic and integrative approach to healing. My work combines biology, psychology, and spirituality to help individuals and groups find meaning, build healthier relationships, and reclaim hope after pain.

Through my professional practice, research, and personal life experiences, I have witnessed that healing is possible and worth it. I believe that life is not random, that each person's existence has purpose, and that when people understand their brain, body, and inner world, they can begin to embrace themselves without judgment.

Philosophy & Approach

At Sacred Space Therapeutic Ministry, health is our natural condition.

Illness is a disruption in the flow of things, a dis-ease, an undue tension, friction or stress. Healing is a return to our natural condition, ease, wholeness, harmony, balance, unity, peace and flow. Healing is the state of being unbroken and undamaged. Professional Experience

My professional journey spans hospital settings, therapeutic practice, research, and community ministry.
